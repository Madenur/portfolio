About Me

Not a typical
career path.

I started competing in hackathons before most people had a job title. That habit — building fast, shipping real things, winning under pressure — became the foundation for everything else.

Over five years I moved between backend engineering, data analytics, innovation management, and AI development — not because I couldn't pick one, but because I kept finding problems worth solving at every level.

When I earned a full scholarship to University of Glasgow for Data Engineering, a visa delay gave me a year to do some of the most interesting work of my career. Now I'm there — and still building.
